The JED-1 Coding Workshop

Upgrade Your Kids.

The Jed-1 programme teaches students computer skill sets while allowing them to create games and projects of their own with the latest technologies, such as Generative AI, making it absolutely fun and interesting to learn.

The Jed-1 programme is created by Jed Wan, who has taught computer classes to kids in various high schools in Penang including Penang Chinese Girls High School, Al Mashoor, Abdullah Munshi, Saujana Indah, and Sheh Shahabuddin.
